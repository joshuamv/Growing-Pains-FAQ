Frequently asked questions

What data do you collect?

Only submitted data through the “Tell your story” form is anonymously collected: Language chosen, text inputted, and age group chosen. We don't link this data back to you. No other data is collected.

What do you do with the collected data?

The data shared is used to display your story anonymously in the story gallery.

Can submitted data be removed?

Yes. You can send a request to take down your story to delete@growingpains.me and we'll get to it as soon as we can. Remember to include the number of the story you want to delete and the reason why you're requesting for it to be deleted.

Is the data submitted really anonymous?

Yes. Keep in mind that we have no control over what you write, so you're responsible about not revealing any details that can be linked back to you if you want it to remain anonymous.

Can I report data I think shouldn’t be displayed here or that might be harmful?

Yes. If you feel like there's content that's harmful or shouldn't be there you can report it at report@growingpains.me and we'll get to it as soon as we can. Remember to include the number of the story you want to report and the reason why you're requesting for it to be deleted.

Why do only some stories have audio?

For a few special stories, I interviewed the people telling them and shared those recordings with their permission for everyone to listen and connect with.

Can I contact you for a professional inquiry?

Sure! You can hit me up at contact@growingpains.me or through my LinkedIn.

Who made Growing Pains?

Growing Pains was designed by Josh Milwer and developed by Yarden Ish-Shalom.
